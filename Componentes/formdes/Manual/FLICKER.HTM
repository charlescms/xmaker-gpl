TFlickerFreeTransition

Unit

TransEff

Declaration

TFlickerFreeTransition = class(TTransitionEffect)

Description

It makes a direct transition, avoiding flickering in complex images. If the source and destination images use different palettes and the video mode is 256 colors paletted, then the palette switching will produce a strange effect until the transition finishes.
You can use the global FlickerFreeTransition instance.
