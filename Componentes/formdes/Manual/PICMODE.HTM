TFCPictureMode

Unit

FormCont

Declaration

TFCPictureMode = (fcpmCenter, fcpmCenterStretch, fcpmStretch, fcpmTile);

Description

These are the different possibilities to draw a picture:
 
fcpmCenter: centers the picture keeping its original size.
fcpmCenterStretch: stretchs the picture maintaining its aspect ratio and centering it.
fcpmStretch: stretchs the picture covering the full area.
fcpmTile: tiles the picture covering the full area.
