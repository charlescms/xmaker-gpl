FormContainer introduction

FormContainer is a a native component designed for Delphi that makes possible to insert a form as a child window into another form.

For example, let's suppose we have designed the Form TMyForm, and we want to insert it into a TFormContainer called MyFormContainer:

var
  AForm: TCustomForm;
begin
  AForm := MyFormContainer.CreateForm(TMyForm);
  MyFormContainer.ShowForm(AForm, True);
end;

The possible applications of this feature are only limited by your imagination You can easily develope very complex interfaces that are much more powerful, robust and user-friendly.

Of course, the above example is a very simple. You can have many forms attached to the same FormContainer, although only one can be showed at the same time, control the alignment of each one, show or hide it with a transition effect, and many more things.
