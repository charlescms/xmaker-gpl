Bugs

There are no known bugs at the present.

Limitations

  1. You have to follow a few simple guidelines to correctly show your forms within a FormContainer.
  2. There are palette conflicts if you use transitions in 256 colors video mode and the source and destination images use different palettes. Set PassSetting to tePaletteDependent to avoid them, or use the same logical palette in all your forms (only those that need a logical palette, of course).
  3. Some controls can not be correctly rendered for the transition, but you can fine-tune them using RegisterTEControl or modifying them (if you have their source code).
